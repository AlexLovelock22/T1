SELL YOUR CAR

Here at Tier One, we understand that selling your car is an important decision. That’s why we offer a seamless, professional service that puts your needs first. Whether you’re looking for a quick sale or seeking the best possible value, our expert team ensures that the entire process is efficient, secure, and hassle-free. With a focus on providing the highest level of customer satisfaction, we make sure your car is treated with the care and attention it deserves.

Sale Process

1

Outright Purchase

Our Outright Purchase service offers a quick, hassle-free way to sell your car. After a fast evaluation, we arrange a viewing and instantly transfer the funds, including clearing any outstanding finance. This service ensures a smooth, same-day transaction, leaving you with cleared funds and no complications.

2

Sale or return

For those seeking a higher return or who prefer selling privately, our brokerage service handles everything. We provide advertising, finance options, and after-sales care, ensuring a quick, secure sale.

3

Brokerage Service

Our competitive commission structure ensures you get the best deal. We prepare your car to the highest standard and advertise it on top platforms like Auto-Trader and Facebook Marketplace for maximum exposure.
