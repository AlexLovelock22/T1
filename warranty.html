WARRANTY

As piece of mind for you, all of our vehicles up to 10 years at purchase date will automatically include a comprehensive Gold level warranty cover with no annual millage restrictions. You will be able to make unlimited number of claims for duration of your policy. The policy will also include a labour charge cover of £80 per hour as standard.
This policy can be used at any VAT registered garage within the UK and Northern Island.

WHAT'S COVERED

Electrics
Alternator, electric window motors, engine cooling fan motors, horn, indicator flasher relay, starter motor & sunroof motor/hydraulics.
Electronic Control Units
The engine management ECU – engine ECU only. (Not covered: Upgraded or revised software & firmware, including software patches or retro-fitted ECUs/computers.)
Braking System
ABS pump & control unit, calipers, master & wheel cylinders, pressure reducing & proportioning valves & vacuum.
Casings
Covers are included only if damage results directly from the failure of a covered component.
Clutch
Clutch cable, master & slave hydraulic cylinders, and pedal assembly.
Cooling System
Water radiator, heater radiator, thermostat, housing, viscous cooling fan couplings, and water pump.
Engine
All internally lubricated parts including camshafts, followers, rockers, crankshaft, bearings, pistons, rings, gears, valves, chains, guides, and seals. (Excludes burnt/pitted valves and valve seats.)
Flywheels & Drive Plates
Flexible drive plate for automatic transmission torque converter, solid & dual mass flywheels & starter ring. Flywheels damaged by clutch friction not covered.
Fuel System
Airflow sensors, fuel gauge sender, pressure regulators, accumulators, fuel pumps (pre/low/high pressure), and injectors (petrol, diesel, single-point).
Gearboxes
Manual, auto, hybrid, 4WD transfer, CVT, actuators, clutch packs, modulator valves, selector forks, shafts, synchronisers, gears, valve blocks.
Ignition System
Camshaft sensors and crankshaft ignition.
Oil Seals & Gaskets
Crankshaft rear oil seal & main gearbox input shaft oil seal are covered. Other seals & gaskets only if leak requires unit removal. (Not covered: Mist/leak not requiring removal.)
Steering Column
Tilt mechanism & couplings, power steering pump, reservoir, pressure pipes, steering & idler boxes, rack and pinion system.
Suspension
Wheel bearings.
Transmission
Differentials (front, rear, center), CV joints, shafts, constant velocity joints, crown wheels & pinions, bearings, bushes, universal joints, propshafts.
Turbocharger
Turbocharger unit only.
Also Covered
Crankshaft pulley, cylinder head gasket, camshaft drive belts & tensioners (covered if last change was per manufacturer’s service schedule).
