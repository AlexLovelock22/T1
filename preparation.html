VEHICLE PREPARATION

100-Hour Car Sale Preparation Process

Our 100-hour process begins with a full pre-purchase inspection to ensure only outstanding vehicles are selected. Each car then undergoes a detailed mechanical check to resolve any issues and optimise performance. The final 60 hours are dedicated to paint correction, interior restoration, and fine detailing, finishing with a strict quality check under precision lighting to guarantee a showroom-ready result.

Pre-Purchase Inspection

We source exclusively from private sellers, avoiding auctions and trade stock to ensure quality and provenance. Vehicles are hand-picked by our team using strict cosmetic and mechanical criteria. Tools like sun guns and paint gauges confirm originality, and any sign of filler, repairs, or mileage tampering leads to disqualificationâ€”ensuring only factory-true examples reach our showroom.

Mechanical Inspection

Every vehicle must pass a thorough 120-point health check performed by model-specific experts. We rectify mechanical faults and replace any consumables with limited life. Further checks include software audits, mileage validation, previous tuning history, and advanced diagnostics. Only once a car is fully cleared does it move on to our cosmetic preparation phase, carried out in-house by our detailing team in Telford.

60-Hour Detailing Preparation Process

We start with a deep decontamination wash, spending up to four hours removing embedded grime from wheels, arches, and engine bays, followed by thorough pre-washing and clay bar treatment. Inside our temperature-controlled detailing studio, each car receives up to 40 hours of multi-stage machine polishing to eliminate swirls, marring, and holograms. Using refined tools and techniques, we achieve a durable, better-than-factory finish while ensuring every surface, from exhaust tips to interiors, is flawlessly presented.
